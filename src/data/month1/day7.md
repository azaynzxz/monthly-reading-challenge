Traffic jams are usually annoying, but here in the city, they are like a moving art gallery called Phool Patti. I watched a huge truck pass by, and it was covered in intricate, detailed paintings of bright flowers, peacocks, and poetic verses. Drivers spend fortunes decorating their vehicles because they want to display their identity. Metal bells hanging from the bumper chimed musically as the truck bounced over the bumps. This truck art turns a dusty, gray road into a vibrant, moving festival, and it demonstrates that you can find beauty in the most ordinary places.

The sun had not risen yet when we drove into the Maasai Mara, which is famous for the spectacular Great Migration. The grass was tall and gold, hiding secrets in its waves. My heart skipped a beat when our driver pointed. Just a few meters away, a lioness was walking silently with her cubs. She looked at us with those calm, yellow eyes before she moved on into the bush. I held my breath the whole time because I was afraid to break the magic of the moment.

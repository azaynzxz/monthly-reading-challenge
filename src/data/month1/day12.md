The music started playing, and I could not sit still. The guitars were upbeat, and the drums had a complex rhythm that forced my feet to move. This was Highlife music, which is the foundation for modern Afrobeat and the sound of pure joy in my country. I danced with strangers in the street, and everyone was smiling and sweating under the hot sun. For a few hours, we had no worries and no deadlines, but we just had the beat.

I visited Mazar-i-Sharif today, and it is an oasis of peace that feels like it belongs in a painting. The shrine there is covered in thousands of intricate blue tiles that reflect the sunlight like a mirror. As I walked through the courtyard, hundreds of white doves flew up into the sky. Legend says that if a grey dove flies here, it turns white because of the holiness of the area. The contrast between those brilliant blue tiles and the pure white birds was breathtaking, making it a true sanctuary in a chaotic world.

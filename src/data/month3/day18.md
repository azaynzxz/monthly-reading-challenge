I am walking through the busy streets of Port-au-Prince, past walls covered in bright, colorful murals. The art tells stories of hope and struggle. I stop to watch a local artist painting a new piece on a concrete wall, using brushes and spray cans. People gather around to watch and comment. The artist works quickly, his hands moving with confidence. Art is everywhere here, on walls, on tap-taps, on everything! It transforms our city into a gallery. This creativity shows that beauty can grow anywhere, even in difficult places.

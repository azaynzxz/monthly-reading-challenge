I am sitting on a wooden bench outside my neighbor's house in Arusha, shaded by a tall Mango tree. The morning sun is just starting to warm the ground. I am helping to roast green coffee beans over a small charcoal fire. The beans crackle and pop, filling the air with a rich, smoky smell. Then we grind them by hand using a heavy stone. My hands are getting tired, but the smell is amazing! Later, we will brew it strong and share it with everyone. This is how coffee should taste, made with patience and care.

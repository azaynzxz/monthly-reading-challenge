I am sitting on my front step, thinking about all the places I have visited through these stories. From the rice fields of Madagascar to the salt flats of Bolivia, I have learned something important. Home is not just a place; it is the feeling you get when you share food, when you work together, when you sit in a circle and talk. Everywhere I went, people showed me that happiness comes from simple things: a warm meal, good company, and traditions passed down through generations. We are all connected by these basic human joys.

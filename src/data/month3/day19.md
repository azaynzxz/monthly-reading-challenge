I am standing in an open field in Thimphu, watching a traditional archery match. The targets are tiny, placed far away. The archers wear traditional dress and shoot with long bamboo bows. When someone hits the target, everyone dances and sings! It is more like a celebration than a competition. I am not playing today, just watching, but the energy is infectious. The mountains surround us, their peaks touching the clouds. This ancient sport brings our community together. It is about skill, but also about joy and tradition.

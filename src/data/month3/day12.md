I am standing in my aunt's small kitchen in Tegucigalpa, which opens to a courtyard with a big Avocado tree. The smell of corn masa fills the air. My aunt is showing me how to pat the dough into perfect circles. Clap, clap, clap, her hands move so fast! I try to copy her, but mine come out lopsided. She laughs and helps me fix it. We cook them on a hot griddle until they puff up. Eating a fresh tortilla with beans is the best breakfast in the world, really!

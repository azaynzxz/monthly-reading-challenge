# Reading Practice Criteria

## Writing Style Characteristics
- No emojis
- No em dashes
- Rich conjunctions (and, but, however, because, while, although, so, yet, therefore)
- Correct grammar throughout
- Educational embedded explanations using "which is...", "called...", "meaning..."
- Cultural context for educational purpose
- First-person perspective for immersive reading
- Present-to-past tense mixing for storytelling flow
- Paraphrase and enrich stories with cultural context based on the country
- Include educational knowledge (history, traditions, local terms, geography) so readers learn while reading
- Add descriptive details, sensory experiences, and emotional reflections to deepen the narrative

---

## Day-Based Progression (Resets Every Month)

Each month follows a progressive difficulty curve from Day 1 to Day 30. The progression resets at the beginning of each new month.

### Text Length Progression
| Day Range | Length | Word Count (approx.) |
|-----------|--------|---------------------|
| Day 1-5   | Short  | 80-120 words        |
| Day 6-10  | Short-Medium | 120-180 words |
| Day 11-15 | Medium | 180-250 words       |
| Day 16-20 | Medium-Long | 250-320 words  |
| Day 21-25 | Long   | 320-400 words       |
| Day 26-30 | Long   | 400-500 words       |

### Vocabulary Complexity Progression
| Day Range | Level | Description |
|-----------|-------|-------------|
| Day 1-5   | Simple | Common everyday vocabulary, basic sentence structures |
| Day 6-10  | Simple-Intermediate | Introduction of some descriptive adjectives and compound sentences |
| Day 11-15 | Intermediate | More varied vocabulary, longer sentences with multiple clauses |
| Day 16-20 | Intermediate-Advanced | Cultural and specialized terms with explanations, complex sentence patterns |
| Day 21-25 | Advanced | Rich vocabulary, sophisticated expressions, embedded educational content |
| Day 26-30 | Advanced | Full complexity with diverse vocabulary, layered storytelling, and deep cultural context |

### Sentence Structure Progression
| Day Range | Structure |
|-----------|-----------|
| Day 1-5   | Simple and compound sentences |
| Day 6-10  | Compound sentences with 1-2 conjunctions |
| Day 11-15 | Complex sentences with relative clauses |
| Day 16-20 | Multiple embedded clauses and explanations |
| Day 21-25 | Sophisticated multi-layered sentences |
| Day 26-30 | Full mastery with varied structures |

---

## Monthly Reset
- Every new month (Month 1, Month 2, Month 3, etc.) starts fresh from Day 1
- Difficulty progression repeats: Short/Simple to Long/Complex
- This allows learners to experience the full progression curve each month
- Topics and countries change, but the learning curve remains consistent

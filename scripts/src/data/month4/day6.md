01
I watched the strange, rust-colored man locked tightly inside the iron cage in my father's courtyard. My shiny golden ball had rolled into his cage, and I desperately wanted it back.

02
I secretly stole the heavy iron key from under my mother's pillow and unlocked the creaking door. The wild man grabbed me, and we fled deep into the dark, mysterious forest where I had to survive by guarding a magical, golden spring.

03
Years later, a fierce war threatened my kingdom, and I rode into the chaotic battlefield on a magnificent horse provided by the wild man. My heart pounded against my ribs, and my hands gripped the cold leather reins tightly as enemy arrows flew past my face.

04
I fought bravely and drove the invading enemies away, but I hid my true identity beneath my heavy iron helmet. My family praised the unknown knight, completely unaware that he was their long-lost son.

05
The wild man's curse was finally broken, revealing him as a mighty and noble king. I learned that true strength is forged through hardship, discipline, and remaining loyal to those who guide us.
